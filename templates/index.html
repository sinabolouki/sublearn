Introduction

Ever had the problem that the subtitle of a movie is 90% of the time too simple; and suddenly you get to a brief sentense with a single word that you don't understand?

What if before you start watching the movie, you would know the words that might be new to you? In that case you would be able to read all the normal sentenses with the comfort you'd expect to have, and when you get to that new word you recieve a discription of it?

This is what Sublearn is all about! We will modify the subtitle in a way that it fits your knowledge of English language; then you'll be able to use that modified version of the subtitle to improve your vocabulary or just to simply watch the movie with more comfort.

How does it work?

You can make your account for free. Then your level of knowledge of English language will be determined using a simple quick test.

From there on, you can upload your subtitles and we will modify it according to your level. In result you get a version of the subtitle that show descriptions of the words that might be new for you.
